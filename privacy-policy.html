Privacy Policy for AI Assistant Extension

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to the developer.

How Data Is Used

  • Text you select on web pages is sent directly to the AI provider you choose (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepSeek), using your own API key.
  • Your API keys are stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local and are never sent to the developer.
  • Chat messages are processed entirely between your browser and the AI provider's servers.

Website Content

The extension reads text you explicitly select on web pages solely to fulfill your requested AI action (translate, summarize, explain, etc.). This content is sent only to your chosen AI provider and is not retained by the developer.

Third-Party Services

This extension may communicate with the following AI provider APIs based on user configuration:

  • OpenAI (openai.com)
  • Anthropic (anthropic.com)
  • DeepSeek (deepseek.com)
  • Moonshot / Kimi (moonshot.cn)
  • Alibaba Qwen (aliyuncs.com)
  • Google Gemini (googleapis.com)
  • Zhipu AI (bigmodel.cn)

Please review each provider's privacy policy for their data practices.
